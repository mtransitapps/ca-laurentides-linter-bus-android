Full description:
This application adds L'Inter des Laurentides (TaCL) buses information to MonTransit.

This app contains the buses schedule and the latest news from www.transportlaurentides.ca and @TACLLaurentides on Twitter.

L'Inter des Laurentides serves L’Ascension, Macaza, Nominingue, Mont-Tremblant, Prévost, Rivière-Rouge et St-Jérôme in Quebec (Canada).

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by the Transport adapté et collectif des Laurentides (TaCL).

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-laurentides-linter-bus-android

This app is not related with the Transport adapté et collectif des Laurentides (TaCL) and Inter des Laurentides.

Permissions:
- Other: required to read news from www.transportlaurentides.ca and Twitter
